About Our Workshop

Building Trust Through Quality Engineering

Sharma Engineering and Workshop, founded by Sudhakar Sharma in 2019, has grown to become one of the most trusted engineering workshops in East Champaran, Bihar. We specialize in manufacturing high-performance agricultural machinery and custom metal fabrication.

Located in Maduahan, Jasauli Patti, Kotwa, we serve farmers and industrial clients across Kotwa, Motihari, and surrounding districts with precision-engineered solutions.
