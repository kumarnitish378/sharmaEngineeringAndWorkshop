Robotics & Autonomous Research

Automating Bihar's Future.

Sharma Engineering and Workshop is currently researching and prototyping advanced off-road rovers. We aim to solve local labor shortages and transport issues with modular robotics.

Phase: Manual RC Control

Manual Off-Road Rover

Research on rugged, high-torque chassis controlled via long-range RC systems. Sharma Workshop is designing these for hazardous environment inspection and heavy lifting in rough agricultural fields.

  • All-Terrain Capability
  • Custom Heavy Payload
Phase: Semiautomatic

Semiautomatic Logistics

Developing assistive navigation and obstacle detection sensors. These rovers help workers at Sharma Engineering and Workshop and client facilities follow paths autonomously for transport and warehouse logistics.

  • Path-Following Tech
  • Safety Collision Sensors
Phase: Fully Automatic

Fully Automatic AI Rover

Integrating computer vision and AI for independent agricultural tasks. Our team is researching autonomous seeding, soil monitoring, and automated farm-to-storage transport solutions.

  • Vision-Based AI
  • GPS Pathfinding
