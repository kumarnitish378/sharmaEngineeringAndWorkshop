Concept Analysis & Value Proposition

This section outlines the core problem addressed by Safe-Tag and demonstrates how the proposed hybrid technology offers a superior solution compared to traditional methods. Interact with the scenario below to understand the real-world impact of the product.

⚠ The Problem

In emergencies involving accidents, missing children, or lost luggage, critical information is often inaccessible. The "Golden Hour" is lost because blood groups, medical history, and emergency contacts are unknown to first responders or Good Samaritans.

💡 The Solution

A hybrid RFID + QR Code tag ecosystem.

QR Code: Instantly accessible by any smartphone user without a specialized app.
RFID (NFC/HF): Highly durable, professional tap-to-read functionality ideal for institutional use (Schools, Hospitals).

Interactive Scenario Testing

Scenario: A child is lost in a crowded market.

The child is too scared to speak or recall phone numbers. Bystanders are unable to help immediately.

With Safe-Tag: A shopkeeper scans the QR code on the child's backpack. Instantly, the parent's phone number appears on their screen. The parent is called within 10 seconds.

PHASE 1

Software Setup & User Registration

Create a simple, secure landing page using a low-cost host (like Hostinger). This portal will allow customers to enter their emergency details (Name, Contact, Blood Group, Allergies).

  • Generate a unique identifier (URL) for each registered user.
  • Implement a "Privacy Toggle" allowing users to hide sensitive data until an "Emergency Mode" is activated.
  • Ensure mobile responsiveness, as scanning will occur via smartphones.
